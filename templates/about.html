    Brief Company Description

    BLUELAND MEDICAL TRADING PLC is a company established and registered to import and distribute Medicines and Medical equipment in Ethiopia. Blue land Trading PLC was incorporated in 2015 per the Commercial Code of Ethiopia with Principal Reg. No. MT/AA/2/0015297/2004 with an initial capital of USD 100,000 which has grown to USD 275,000 since it went to business.


    BLUELAND MEDICAL TRADING PLC was established with a seven shareholders who have different professional mix, but with extensive experience in the business working for different similar or competing companies. Which by the way gives a competitive advantage as the partners have the customer base, demands, test and psychology of the entire market. The other competitive advantage we have is the customer contacts and profile that our partners come with makes it easier to promote, sale and distribute with relations establish over the years.


    The company has started its activities by importing and distributing medical equipment and supplies used for different surgery eye procedures. Blue land is a strong company with a stronger managing team to serve the market in better and efficient ways. At present, apart from distrusting medicine and surgical disposables, the company works with Nurtexs textile Ve medical- Turkey which are certified National and international private companies which have significant surgical pack markets in the world. In addition to this exclusive agent we have got second agent assignment of one Indian ophthalmic medical devices and equipments manufacturing company, the name so called Appasamy Associates. In addition to these above two companies we have two companies agreement .and they are submitted all necessary documents to FMHACA for document analyses. Hope within the next few months we will finish registration and start our business actively and effectively with the following companies .


    Name and their country of origin of these companies are:

    • 1. Variteks Orthopaedic manufacturing company-Turkey
    • 2. Micro surgical medical devices manufacturing company-India

    Blue Land is established by determined shareholders, sufficient capital and vast expertise. Working with us will benefit both the manufacturing and Blue Land. As the Ethiopian business environment is opening up a greater opportunity for those registered under and with a local company, it is our interest to work with companies manufacturing ophthalmic and dental products. Blue land is a company which is aiming to grow in the short term to become one of the leading companies that will bring change to the health of the public. We intend to delivering qualitative products and service to the public, this will differentiate our company from other competing companies.
